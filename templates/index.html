GeoTracer:

Interactive, Crowd-Sourced Migration Map


Add Your Story!

How to add your story:

Submit up to five points from your family's history, begining with where you live now.
Each point should represent a location of significance for that generation, whether they were born there, lived most of their lives, or identify strongly with that place for any reason.
You must add at least two points to create a submission. After adding all of your points, use the submit button to trace them together for you and add your submission to the map. The resulting map will combine with other users' histories to show an interactive crowd-sourced visualization of how disparate origins combine together to create a shared story.
